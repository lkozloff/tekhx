What's a "slam-dunk?"

A slam-dunk is a rapid presentation style. At the beginning of the session all presenters will be invited on stage to give a 1 minute (or less) synopsis of their presentation (the "slam"). Immediately following all presenters and particpants break out in to tables where the presenters give their 20 minute presentation (the "dunk").

The presentations are repeated 2-3 times with a new group of participants each time.

So, you want to present?

First, thanks! We couldn't have a successful conference without people willing to share their knowledge with their peers. If you've never presented before, double congratulations. Your first presentation is going to go great.

The details

We have three types of slots available: 20 minute slam-dunk sessions (see sidebar), 60 minute topic presentations and 2 hour workshops for those that realy want to dig in.

Good topics will include some practical tips, some additional resources to explore afterwards and maybe even some hands on time.

The best tip of all is to ask yourself: "If I didn't know this topic, would I want to see this presentation?"
