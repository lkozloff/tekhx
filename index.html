So what's this all about?

Started in 2014, TEKHin - Technology (TE) Integration (in) in Cambodia (KH) - is a conference created and run by international school teachers for international school teachers. The organising schools for this year are:

  • International School of Phnom Penh (ISPP)
  • Hope International School
  • Liger Learning Center
  • Logos International School
  • Northbridge International School Cambodia (NISC)

Teachers from many schools in Phnom Penh and from around Cambodia have been invited to join this core group and come together to share the wealth of amazing practices happening in our classrooms to better support our students' learning with technology.
