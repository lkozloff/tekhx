Below is a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).

  1. When will conference registrations be opened?
    We generally open registration for attendees 4 weeks before the conference.

  2. What if I've never given a presentation before?
    We love beginners! If you're feeling confident, don't hesitate to submit for a longer session, but first time presenters might enjoy the fast pace of our "slam-dunk" style talks. 20 minutes isn't a lot of time to fill, but it's enough to get you out there at your first conference!
